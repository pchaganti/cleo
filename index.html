A notebook / sheets of paper A pen How to get started: Step 1: Decide on how long you want to get bored. 15-60 minutes. 5-12 sessions with each session 3-5 minutes long. Step 2: Write down on your notebook a list of numbers from 1 to X. (X = the number of sessions you chose). 1.54:59 Step 3: Start the timer, do nothing and get bored. Step 4: When each session ends, think of one word that captures the theme of that session. It can be a noun, a verb, an adjective; an emotion, an action, some random vocabulary. Your choice! Write that word down next to the numbered list. 1.54:59 Distraction That is all! Note: These are rules I follow when I am doing the challenge. Feel free not to. No usage of pen and paper besides writing down the keyword. By all means think but let your thoughts slip. Do not write them down. The goal is to be unproductive. Try not to doze off during the challenge. Try not to engage in any other activities besides sitting or pacing. Pause the timer if you need to drink water or adjust your posture. I have been trying this for a couple of days. Turns out getting bored for an hour is way harder than, say, focusing on a textbook and study for an hour.
