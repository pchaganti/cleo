Luckily, there's a solution to the problem: Start blogging! There's no better way. Or rather, it's the only way. We discover that what we considered trash is someone else’s treasure. We understand that many people identify with what we thought we were alone in feeling. We find ourselves by connecting with others. My first blog in Swedish started as a complete mess and then slowly but surely developed into a pure tech blog. Although technology were and still is a big interest of mine, I could never have figured out in advance that that was the direction my blogging would take. It came naturally thanks to starting to blog. This blog is my first "serious" one in English. Maybe it will continue like this, or maybe I'll start a separate blog about writing online. I don't know; time will tell...
